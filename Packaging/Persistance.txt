Persistance

    - Persistance programs start when the system starts
        - Backdoors -> maintain our access
        - Keyloggers -> spy on target.
        - Reporters -> send report on startup.
        - etc...
    
    cmd prompt:
        reg add HKCU\Software\Microsoft\windows\CurrentVersion\Run /v (name) /t REG_SZ /d (file location)
        ex:
            reg add HKCU\Software\Microsoft\windows\CurrentVersion\Run /v test /t REG_SZ /d "C:/test.exe"
